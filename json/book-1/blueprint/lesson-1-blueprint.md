Do You...?

**[Block: Paragraph]**
Style: intro
To make simple direct questions in the present, we use "**Do + person + action?**". 
We can ask about actions, routines, and preferences.

**[Block: Advanced Grammar]** Basic Structure
Details: We use DO combined with I, You, They and We

| Use | Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Question | Do + person + action? | Do you study English? |
| Short Positive Answer | Yes, person + do. | Yes, I do. |
| Short Negative Answer | No, person + don't. | No, I don't |

**[Block: Advanced Grammar]** More ways to answer
Explanation: Use a short answer first. Then you can add extra information.
Details: Keep the same answer pattern every time in this part.

| Question | Short Answer | Long Answer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Do you study English? | Yes, I do. | Yes, I do. I study at night. |
| Do you work? | No, I don't. | No, I don't. I study in the morning. |
| Do you like music? | Yes, I do. | Yes, I do. I like pop music. |
